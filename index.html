About Dr. Srinu and Balaji Hospital

Dr. M Srinu is a distinguished cardiologist and general physician with over 18 years of medical experience. A dedicated Government Doctor, he has served since 2008 with compassion and clinical excellence. His qualifications include MBBS, M.D. (General Medicine), and D.M. (Cardiology), reflecting his deep expertise and commitment to patient well-being.

Under his leadership, Srinu’s Balaji Hospital has grown into a trusted name in Hyderabad for heart and general health care. Known for ethical practice, modern facilities, and personal attention, the hospital provides patients with quality treatment and genuine care — where every heartbeat truly matters.
